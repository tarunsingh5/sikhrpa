Education • Safety • Community

Our Mission

The specific purpose of this corporation is to provide public education and training on firearm safety, proper firearm handling and maintenance, and the historical and technical variations of diverse firearm types, thereby promoting responsible ownership and community safety.

Who We Are

We are a California Nonprofit Public Benefit Corporation organized exclusively for educational and charitable purposes under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code.

The 4 Rules of Safety

Education is your first line of defense. Memorize these rules before you ever touch a firearm:

Rule English Focus Punjabi Translation
1 Assume it's loaded ਹਮੇਸ਼ਾ ਇਹ ਮੰਨ ਕੇ ਚੱਲੋ ਕਿ ਹਥਿਆਰ ਲੋਡ (Loaded) ਹੈ
2 Safe direction ਪਿਸਟਲ ਦਾ ਮੂੰਹ ਹਮੇਸ਼ਾ ਸੁਰੱਖਿਅਤ ਦਿਸ਼ਾ ਵੱਲ ਰੱਖੋ
3 Trigger discipline ਆਪਣੀ ਉਂਗਲ ਟਰਿੱਗਰ (Trigger) ਤੋਂ ਦੂਰ ਰੱਖੋ
4 Know your target ਆਪਣੇ ਨਿਸ਼ਾਨੇ ਅਤੇ ਉਸਦੇ ਪਿੱਛੇ ਦੀ ਜਗ੍ਹਾ ਨੂੰ ਜਾਣੋ
